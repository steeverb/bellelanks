§ 01

About

I'm a design‑savvy content strategist, editor, and writer based in Brooklyn. I care about clear, concise prose, the craft of editing, and the editorial systems that make brand publications feel like magazines instead of marketing.

I've led editorial at Razorfish (Samsung Explore), founded and edited Huge's Magenta, consulted with SY Partners, and edited books for TED and AARP. Before that — and still today — I write features and book reviews for The Wall Street Journal, Bloomberg Businessweek, WIRED, and Fast Company.
